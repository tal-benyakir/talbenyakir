Tal Ben Yakir is a journalist and photographer based in Paris & Amsterdam. She covers geopolitics, technology and the far right, and is especially passionate about the intersection of politics in everyday culture, from fashion to fine arts.

She has a master's degree from Sciences Po Paris in International Security.
