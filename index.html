← Home

Tal Ben Yakir is a journalist and photographer based in Paris. She covers populism, disinformation, and the geopolitics of technology, with work published in Vice Netherlands and Vice France.

She is currently completing a master's degree at Sciences Po Paris (PSIA), concentrating in Global Risks and the Middle East.

Her photography spans documentary, fashion, and institutional work, including projects for Foam Museum and Meervaart Theater.
